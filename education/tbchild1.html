learning Colors

Teaching colors to kids is best done through play and everyday life experiences. Many children easily learn their colors as parents and caregivers point out color attributes on the objects in children’s environment (e.g., “Throw me the green ball” or “Do you want the red shirt or the yellow shirt?”). This board features fun, interactive, and developmentally appropriate activities for helping kids to learn their colors.

Colors is one of our fun and simple educational games for kids aged 2-5 years old. The colors games will introduce the basic colors for toddlers to your baby. As it turns out, colors and shapes are a key way that we describe and categorize our world. Children will notice that a red flower is different from a yellow one, and that a round bread roll has a different shape than a square slice of bread.
